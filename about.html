About

Hi, I'm Michael.

I'm a coach for people who take their growth seriously — individuals working on themselves, and leaders working on their teams.

MC

My story

My path into coaching came from years of asking the same question in different forms: what does it actually take for a person — or a team — to change? I've spent that time working alongside individuals navigating transitions and leaders under real pressure, and the lessons that stuck were the simple ones — clarity of intent, honest reflection, small commitments, repeated often.

How I work

I believe coaching should be useful first and inspiring second. Our sessions are structured but never rigid: we'll get specific about what you want, name what's getting in the way, and build the practices and decisions that move you forward. I bring directness when it helps, patience when it's needed, and a strong bias toward what's actually working in your life or business.

Who I work with

I work with two kinds of clients. Individuals — people committed to their own personal development, often during a transition, a stretch goal, or a season where the way they've been operating no longer fits who they're becoming. And companies — leadership teams and managers who want sharper thinking, better conversations, and a real shift in how they show up for their people. Group sessions and workshops are tailored to each engagement.

If any of this resonates, the easiest next step is a short intro call. No pressure, no pitch — just a conversation.
