Coaching vs Mentoring

Understanding the distinction

Many leaders confuse the two — and both have a place. The workshop draws a sharp line between mentoring (giving answers from experience) and coaching (asking questions to surface them).

Dimension Coaching Mentoring
Core approach Inquiry-based — asks powerful questions to elicit internal solutions. Directive — provides advice, answers, and models based on personal experience.
Focus Development of self-reliance, structure, and future action. Knowledge transfer and navigating existing corporate pathways.
Ownership The individual owns the outcome and defines their own action steps. The mentor shares a blueprint or suggestions for the individual to follow.
Corporate Benefits

Cultivating growth and engagement

Integrating a coaching culture into your organization correlates directly with sustained business performance and employee satisfaction. By training leaders to adopt a coaching mindset, companies unlock real advantages:

Evoking active listening

Shift the corporate communication standard from giving immediate advice to deeply understanding employee perspectives and the challenges underneath them.

Autonomy and accountability

When employees set their own self-directed actions, their engagement and psychological investment in the outcome scale exponentially.

Retention and happiness

Employees who feel heard, valued, and empowered experience higher workplace fulfillment — fostering a collaborative culture that drives long-term retention.

Workshop Agenda

Structured for adult learning

The curriculum is optimized for adult learning patterns through high-frequency feedback loops. Three connected modules, all hands-on.

01

Foundations & mental preparation

Overcoming the default reflex to be an advice-giver. Preparing the mind to hold a neutral, supportive space for team members and tolerate the silence required for real reflection.

02

Core coaching frameworks

Introducing structural dialogue tools, powerful open-ended questioning habits, and active listening metrics — the practical building blocks of a coaching conversation.

03

Practical application & feedback loops

Multiple live practice rounds where leaders coach peers on real scenarios, receiving immediate, actionable feedback to lock in their newly developed skills before they leave the room.
