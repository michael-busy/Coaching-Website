Current Role

Product Lead — B2C at Solaris SE

As Product Lead for B2C at Solaris SE, I'm responsible for our customer-facing banking solutions — from onboarding through to the apps themselves. The role spans commercial performance, continuous delivery, and coordination across many internal stakeholders, external partners, and customers.

In September 2024, Solaris SE migrated 1.1 million credit cards from LBB. I led the build of the migration app, ran the migration itself, and have continued to expand the financial product offering inside the app since.

Building features is only half the job. The other half is keeping the product healthy — reviewing customer feedback, fixing bugs, paying down tech debt, running maintenance — all while shipping new functionality and managing priorities.

Selected work

Two projects I'm proud of

Apple & Google Pay integration

No one in the company had previously integrated Apple or Google Pay, and there's no sandbox environment for Germany. Under time pressure, we built the knowledge from scratch — researching, testing, iterating, and working directly with Apple.

We got certified ahead of the migration and now offer both payment methods to 1.1 million customers.

Customer onboarding

Onboarding a banking customer means collecting significant personal data while remaining compliant with regulators. I owned the onboarding flows across online and physical-branch channels, each with their own requirements.

The launch required aligning approvals across legal, marketing, data protection, Anti-Financial Crime, and customer support — and unlocked new portfolio growth and revenue.
